About Us

Sondgeroth Pool Construction is a family-owned and operated business with the same personal integrity as when it began back in '82! We are a full service company, trained and certified. A satisfied customer base is our goal, so service is our #1 priority. We strive to be clear and simple, and if you wish to discuss anything further, owners Zack and Herb are always available to answer any of your questions. We look forward to doing business with you!

Mission

Vision

Herb Sondgeroth founded this business back in 1983. He worked occasionally for a friend that owned an excavating company, and this triggered a thought. Why not start his own business using the skills he learned while on site. Skip ahead 30 years, and now he is a successful business man with a network of loyal customers and friends.

Our mission is to bring entertainment, relaxation, and knowledge to your backyard. Our prices are lower than other's are willing to offer, and we can guarantee your neighbors' will be jealous. We build true relationships with our clients. We will guide you through the process with our experience, and your vision. We build dreams you can literally jump into.

Our vision is your vision coming to life. Experts in the craft, we know a thing or two about what works and what doesn't. Let us explain imitations, and provide alternatives to the obstacles we are likely to face. As a team, we can plan and execute a project thinking of the future. Ultimately, providing a place for you and your family to enjoy for years to come.
